Sliced Bread Magazine

As layout editor for my college literary magazine, Sliced Bread, I designed our semiannual 70-page issues while helming a 10-person layout staff committee.

I utilized InDesign and Photoshop to create a sleek and professional, yet playful, magazine that could attractively showcase our talented contributors at the accompanying launch parties, which were attended by hundreds of students. Besides laying out dozens of spreads through InDesign masters and templates, I also crafted our covers (top two images) based on art submissions, determined placement and flow of different pieces, copyedited the magazine, and occasionally updated our font choices for fresher looks.
