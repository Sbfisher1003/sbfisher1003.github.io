Love Data

The University of Chicago Library runs an annual "Love Data" week around Valentine's Day, featuring workshops, lectures, and fun study breaks.

For this event series, I designed a multi-platform marketing campaign using original designs. I played off the two eponymous themes to create a binary-code heart simple enough to scale down to social media, yet sufficiently complex to occupy an oversize banner.

In addition to more traditional marketing items, like social-media graphics, email banners, and print flyers, I also crafted designs for the events themselves: buttons for a study break, and an informational handout on Library services.

My designs were so well-received that they have been reused every year since.
