Love Data

I designed a multi-platform marketing campaign for an annual "Love Data" week featuring workshops, lectures, & study breaks.

For this event series, I played off the two eponymous themes to create a binary-code heart on Illustrator simple enough to scale down to social media, yet sufficiently complex to fill up an oversize banner.

In addition to more traditional marketing items (social-media graphics, email banners, print flyers) I also crafted designs for the events themselves: buttons for a study break, and an informational handout on Library services.

My social-media and event designs have been turned into templates and reused every year since.
